Reply response tone guide

Emails 1–3 replies

Warm but soft. Listen first. Don't pitch yet. Build trust. Resume from next email after 3 days if conversation fizzles.

Emails 4–5 replies

Hot lead. Answer their questions directly. Guide toward the signup link. Resume after 3-5 days if fizzles.

Emails 6–7 replies

Hottest leads. They've been reading for weeks. Make it easy. Send the link directly. Move to monthly nurture if fizzles.

All replies — rules

Respond within 1 hour. Pause the sequence immediately. Log in HubSpot — tag which email triggered the reply.
